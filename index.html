About AI Club

The AI Club at Dhanalakshmi Srinivasan University is a community of passionate students and faculty dedicated to advancing knowledge and skills in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

We organize workshops, hackathons, guest lectures, and collaborative projects to provide hands-on experience and foster innovation.

Whether you are a beginner or an expert, our club welcomes you to learn, share, and grow in the exciting field of AI.
